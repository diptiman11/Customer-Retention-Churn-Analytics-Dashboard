# 📊 Customer-Retention-Churn-Analytics-Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by telecom companies, directly impacting revenue and customer lifetime value. This project focuses on analyzing customer churn patterns using Power BI to identify the key factors influencing customer attrition and provide actionable business insights.

The dashboard analyzes customer demographics, service usage, contract details, customer support interactions, and pricing factors to uncover the primary reasons behind churn and help businesses improve customer retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Telecom companies invest heavily in acquiring customers, making retention crucial for long-term profitability.

This project aims to answer the following questions:

- Why are customers leaving?
- Which customer segments are most likely to churn?
- How do pricing, contracts, and customer service affect churn?
- Which customer groups should be targeted for retention campaigns?
- What actions can reduce customer attrition?

## 📂 Dashboard Pages & Analysis

### 1. Executive Overview

The executive dashboard provides a high-level summary of customer churn.

**Key Insights:**
- Overall churn rate is **26.86%**
- Total customers analyzed: **6,687**
- Churned customers: **1,796**
- Competitor-related issues are the largest contributor to churn
- Month-to-month contracts account for the majority of customers

---

### 2. Customer Demographics Analysis

This page focuses on churn behavior across age groups and senior citizens.

#### Findings

- Senior customers exhibit significantly higher churn rates.
- Customers above 65 years show increasing churn trends.
- Younger customers demonstrate relatively stable retention.

#### Business Insight

Senior citizens may require personalized customer support and retention programs.

---

### 3. Contract & Pricing Analysis

This dashboard investigates the relationship between contract types, monthly charges, and churn.

#### Findings

##### Contract Distribution

| Contract Type | Customers |
|--------------|-----------|
| Month-to-Month | 3.41K |
| Two Year | 1.80K |
| One Year | 1.48K |

##### Churn Drivers

- Month-to-month customers are more likely to churn.
- Customers with higher monthly charges show increased churn tendencies.
- Long-term contracts significantly improve retention.

#### Business Insight

Encouraging customers to move to annual or multi-year plans can reduce churn.

---

### 4. Data Usage & Unlimited Plan Analysis

This page evaluates customer churn based on data consumption and unlimited data plans.

#### Findings

| Unlimited Data Plan | Customers | Churn Rate |
|--------------------|-----------|-----------|
| No | 2,193 | 16.10% |
| Yes | 4,494 | 32.11% |

#### Business Insight

Heavy data users may be receiving more attractive offers from competitors, increasing churn risk.

---

### 5. International Usage Analysis

This dashboard examines churn behavior among customers using international services.

#### Findings

- Customers with international service activity exhibit different churn patterns.
- International plan users may require tailored retention strategies.
- Geographic and usage-based segmentation improves targeting accuracy.

#### Business Insight

Specialized international plans and loyalty benefits can help retain high-value customers.

---

### 6. Account Tenure Analysis

This analysis explores how customer loyalty changes over time.

#### Findings

- New customers show the highest churn rates.
- Churn decreases as account tenure increases.
- Long-term customers are significantly more loyal.

#### Business Insight

The first few months after customer acquisition are critical for retention efforts.

## 💡 Recommendations

### 1. Improve Competitive Positioning
- Offer targeted retention discounts.
- Introduce loyalty rewards programs.
- Benchmark competitor pricing regularly.

### 2. Strengthen Customer Support
- Improve customer service training.
- Reduce response and resolution times.
- Monitor customer satisfaction metrics.

### 3. Target High-Risk Segments
- Focus on senior customers.
- Monitor high-usage customers.
- Create personalized retention campaigns.

### 4. Encourage Long-Term Contracts
- Promote annual subscriptions.
- Provide incentives for contract upgrades.
- Reduce dependency on month-to-month plans.

### 5. Enhance Early Customer Experience
- Improve onboarding processes.
- Increase engagement during the first six months.
- Proactively address service concerns.

---

## 📌 Conclusion

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of customer churn behavior in the telecom industry. The findings reveal that competition, customer service quality, pricing, contract structure, and customer demographics are the primary factors driving churn.

By leveraging these insights, organizations can develop targeted retention strategies, reduce customer attrition, and improve long-term business performance.
